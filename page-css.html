Devil Fruits

Synopsis

Devil fruits (from the show "One Piece")are special fruits that, if consumed, grants the person super powers. The only downside of the effects is that you will no longer have the ability to touch or swim in sea water.Your powers will also be nullified by sea prism stone or haki in some cases(see Logia)

Types Of Devil Fruits

There are three types of devil fruits: Zoan, Mythical, Logia, and paramecia. Zoan devil fruits allow the user to turn into animals whilst Mythical Devil fruits allow the user to transform into mythical creatures or other things. Logia devil fruits turns the user into a certain element. In most cases, you will be intagible to all attacks except for water based attacks, sea prism stone, and a certain type of haki. Paramecia devil fruits are a little different.Instead of focusing on a specific thing, the powers they grant could be anything that hasn't been mentioned.
